Food Safety Guidelines

Essential tips for safe food consumption

Check Labels

Always verify FSSAI license numbers, manufacturing dates, and expiry information.

Buy from Trusted Sources

Purchase from licensed retailers and check for proper storage conditions.

Report Issues

Report suspicious or unsafe products to FSSAI or local food safety authorities.

Proper Storage

Store food products according to manufacturer instructions and local climate conditions.

Visual Inspection

Check for signs of tampering, unusual colors, or damaged packaging.

Use Our Detector

Upload product images to our AI detector for instant authenticity verification.
